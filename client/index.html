Years of Tradition

Since 2014

Crafting Memories, One Bowl at a Time

Ramen House began as a small street cart in Shibuya, Tokyo. Our founder, Chef Takeshi, dedicated years to master the art of broth preparation. His philosophy is simple: source the freshest local ingredients and honor the traditional Japanese techniques.

We bring that exact Shibuya street cart recipe straight to your neighborhood, blending traditional umami tastes with modern hospitality and high-end restaurant service.
